Heyyyy


This page has amazing pictures of my pets.


In my time on this earth I have had chickens, parakeets, bunnies, dogs, turtles, fish, and only one cat. Normally, I am not a cat person. I hated them growing up and they hated me. Last summer, I was volunteering at a shelter, and after finishing the normal duties of walking and playing with dogs, feeding and watering them, and bathing them, I decided to go see the kittens. Adelynn was 8 weeks old and was crammed into a tiny cage with 6 other kittens. I got her out and asked if she wanted to come home with me. She meowed and reached for my neck to hug me. She is over a year old now and still hugs me every morning.
